The decision rules, in full

Rule 1 · Leakage

Leakage status of Yes, Unclear, or Not assessed forces Tier 3 until a leakage audit resolves it.

Rule 2 · Both critical gates fail

Direct validation = ND and Benchmark = ND together force Tier 3.

Rule 3 · One critical gate fails

Either Direct validation = ND or Benchmark = ND caps the case at Tier 2.

Rule 4 · Non-critical ND

Input transparency = ND or Accessibility = ND blocks Tier 1 but does not by itself trigger Tier 3.

Rule 5 · Action ceiling

All four gates P, but the action is hypothesis, mechanism, or benchmark work → capped at Tier 2.

Rule 6 · Tier 1

All four gates P and the action is operational decision support → Tier 1, bounded to the stated conditions.

Rule 7 · Remaining partial evidence

Any remaining case, such as one or more Partial ratings with no ND and leakage status No, is assigned Tier 2.

Executable companion to the readiness rubric (Supplementary Table S5) for “AI-driven grass genomics: an evidence-guided roadmap from bioinformatics resources to breeding decisions”. Ratings are use-case-specific and are not rankings of papers, tools, developers, or overall scientific quality. Tiers are bounded to the stated species / evidence domain, task, and intended user action, and reflect the evidence snapshot of 8 July 2026; preprints and repositories should be rechecked before reuse.
